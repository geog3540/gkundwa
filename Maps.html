Final Project - Effects of Marijuana legalization on the Overall and Chronic homelessness crisis issue 2013 - 2023

Description

The goal of this project is to see if there is a correlation between legalizing recreational marijuana and increase in homelessness. For some people the use of marijuana can lead to substance abuse. Also one cause of chronic homelessness is the substance abuse. Therefore, I want to investigate if there is pattern with legalizing marijuana and increase in homelessness over time.

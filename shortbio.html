Gabin Kundwa Transportation Planner

I am currently a graduate student in urban and regional planning with a concentration in transportation planning. At the same time, I am an assistant hall coordinator for Petersen Residence Hall. I have a diverse background, having worked with the consulting firm AECOM during the summer of 2023, where I contributed to various projects, including the writing of a technical report on the performance of Zero Emission Buses for Metro Transit. Additionally, I am currently working on an Affordable Housing Action Plan for the city of Dubuque. In both work settings, I have participated in creating transportation and housing studies, with a primary focus on equity and affordability. I have utilized analytical and planning skills, such as GIS, to create demographic maps and allocate suitable parcels for Transit-Oriented Developments. My strengths lie in my ability to work as a team player, constant learner, and my strong problem-solving skills. I am particularly interested in multimodal transportation and long-range planning, and I am enthusiastic about employing innovative approaches, such as data science, to make transportation projects cost-effective, reliable, equitable, and environmentally friendly. I am seeking a company where I can contribute to solving the world's most complex issues and continue to grow professionally.
